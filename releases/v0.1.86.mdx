### September 26th, 2023

We are excited to announce that a new release of our CLI is now available, bringing some exciting updates and improvements to SDF.

### What's New?
- deps now go to parameter of dep analysis to avoid duplicate analyzing dependencies
- Updated support of assembly references to make referencing (twice) idempotent 
- Initial support of PySpark
- Continued Spark dialect support bug fixes and updates
- Introduced BigQuery grammar support for procedural statements
- Added procedure tests 
- Fixed BigQuery keywords 
- General bug fixes and stability improvements
