### April 5, 2024

We are excited to announce that a new release of our CLI is now available, bringing new docs,
 improvements, and bug fixes to SDF. Interested in our product roadmap or want to 
discuss features? Reach out to your SDF point of contact to provide any points of feedback!

### New Macro Processing Docs:
- [Jinja in SDF](../guide/macro-processing/jinja)
- [Jinja Variables](../guide/macro-processing/jinja-variables)

### General bug fixes and stability improvements:
- SDF Cache updates and bug fixes
- General Snowflake Table Provider Bug Fixes
- Warning updates for local-running unsupported dialects
- Clear troubleshooting error reporting for Redshift `sdf run`
