### March 29nd, 2024

We are excited to announce that a new release of our CLI is now available, bringing
 improvements and bug fixes to SDF. Interested in our product roadmap or want to 
discuss features? Reach out to your SDF point of contact to provide any points of feedback!

### Snowflake Credentials Error Responses
SDF now provides updated error reporting for authenticating to Snowflake. When following the steps to 
authenticate to Snowflake if the incorrect username or password was provided for the account, the following 
error response will be displayed:

``` bash Wrong User/Pass
Please enter your Snowflake password: Password123
    Warning Failed to validate your Snowflake credentials: Failed to authenticate. Error code: 390100. Message: Incorrect username or password was specified.
            - Check that your credentials are correct and that you have access to the specified resources.
    Confirm Saved Snowflake credentials
   Finished auth in 4.263 secs
```


### General bug fixes and stability improvements:
- SDF Cache updates and bug fixes
- Support for GitHub repos as references in SDF Workspaces
- AWS Workspace Credentials Error Response
