### June 9th, 2023

We are excited to announce that this series of releases to our CLI bring some exciting fixes, updates, and improvements to SDF.

### What's New?
- Introduced support for `null` in array literals and array concatenation (i.e. `SELECT ARRAY[1] || ARRAY[2]`)
- Lineage and propagation improvements and bug fixes 
- Patched issue where SDF errors pointed to the wrong source file
- Bug fixes and stability improvements
