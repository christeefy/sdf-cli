### May 30th, 2023

We are excited to announce that this series of releases to our CLI bring some exciting fixes, updates, and improvements to SDF.

### What's New?
- Additional generic testing added
- Introduced support for UDTF left join unnest, cross join unnest, unnest with empty array, unnest with ordinality, and simplest unnest
- Added support for row constructors 
- Fixed non-deterministic tests
- Added support for Pyspark frontend 
- Bug fixes and stability improvements
