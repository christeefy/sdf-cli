### June 6th, 2023

We are excited to announce that this series of releases to our CLI bring some exciting fixes, updates, and improvements to SDF.

### What's New?
- Redshift `ilike` lineage and propagation support
- Improved handling of overlapping `includes` statements across workspace, catalog, schema, and table yaml blocks
- Support for recursive tables and self joins
- Bug fixes and stability improvements
