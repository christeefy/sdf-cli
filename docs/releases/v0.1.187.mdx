### March 22nd, 2024

We are excited to announce that a new release of our CLI is now available, bringing new docs,
 improvements, and bug fixes to SDF. Interested in our product roadmap or want to 
discuss features? Reach out to your SDF point of contact to provide any points of feedback!

### Bring your Jinja
SDF now supports macro processing with Jinja and environment variables. This empowers users to harness 
the full potential of dynamic templating for their workspaces and transformations. 
Jinja offers unparalleled flexibility in enabling DRY patterns and enhancing 
code readability.

### New Docs!
- [SDF Cloud Deployment](/cloud/deployment)
- [Macro Processing With SDF](/guide/macro-processing/overview)
- [Environment Variables](/guide/macro-processing/jinja-variables)

### General bug fixes and stability improvements:
- Support for Jinja Macros
- SDF Test Updates and Stability
- Fix `sdf push` panic issues and 
- Handle cyclic dependency cases with Snowflake
- Additional Snowflake authentication ID support 
- Improved error handling and responses for authentication to Snowflake
