### June 30th, 2024

We are excited to announce that a new minor release of our CLI is now available, bringing
 improvements, new features, and bug fixes to SDF. Interested in our product roadmap or want to 
discuss features? Reach out to your SDF point of contact to provide any points of feedback!

### Documentation

[Snowflake Seeds Guide](/integrations/snowflake/seeds) This new guide provides information on
 the seeds mechanism that helps upload raw data files (e.g. csv files) to Snowflake as tables.

### General bug fixes and stability improvements:
- Added `add` and `delete` button optiosn for workspace credentials
- Applied updates to topological sort and cycle detection
- Fix error with locally defined sources not having source providers
